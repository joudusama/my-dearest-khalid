My Dearest Khalid

In twilight's gentle embrace, my dearest love, Where shadows dance, and stars above. A whispered promise in the moonlit air, Our timeless tale, beyond compare. Through sepia dreams of yesteryear, Your name, a melody in my ear. Love's sweet refrain, a timeless song, In ancient echoes, we belong. Engraved in the parchment of time, Our love, a vintage, sweet and prime. A tapestry woven with threads of gold, Endless stories of love unfold. My dearest Khalid, in this bygone scene, You are my eternal, forever queen. A love that ages, yet never grows old, In the book of us, a story told. Amidst the pages of our love's old lore, You, my dearest, I forever adore.
